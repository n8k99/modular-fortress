```lisp

;; AFTER:
```

```lisp

;; AFTER:
(db-get-tasks-by-filter :goal-id goal-id :limit 50)
```

6. **api-get "/api/af64/tasks" with various filters (lines ~715, ~764)** -> Per D-06:
