## 1. Core Visual Pillars

DragonPunk Solar rests on **five essential pillars**. Every image should embody at least three:

### ☀️ **Solar Foundation**
- **High contrast** — searing whites, intense highlights, defined shadows
- **Dramatic sunlight shadows** — figures partially veiled, clarity in brightness
- **Radiant ambition** — visual tension, nothing purely dim or overcast
- **Sun-bleached grandeur** — weathered elegance, sun-kissed luxury
- **Heat haze and dry surfaces** — reflections everywhere, shimmering horizons

### ⚡ **Cyberpunk Overlay**
- **Solar signage** — holographic, shimmering, in-world languages powered by sun mirrors
- **Augmented reality elements** — floating UI, data streams, HUD overlays
- **Dense urban verticality** — towers with solar sails, catwalks, layered city depths
- **Tech embedded in everything** — photovoltaic panels, mirrors, implants
- **Corporate/guild presence** — logos, propaganda, surveillance drones

### 🐉 **Fantasy Integration**
- **Dragons as apex beings** — not pets, not monsters: ancient intelligences basking in light
- **Magic as technology** — runes that function like solar circuits, spell-crystals
- **Medieval architecture evolved** — gothic spires with solar arrays, castle-solar farms
- **Mythic scale** — dragons, ancient structures, cosmic scope
- **10,500 years of layered history** — ruins beneath ruins beneath sunlit cities

### 🚀 **Orbis Worldbuilding**
- **Colony ship heritage** — generation ships, uploaded consciousnesses
- **Post-scarcity tensions** — abundance with artificial scarcity
- **Faction aesthetics** — each guild/corp has distinct visual language
- **Temporal depth** — technology from multiple eras coexisting
- **Mind/machine blur** — digital ghosts, backup minds, consciousness transfer

### 🎬 **Cinematic Quality**
- **Lens flare optional** — adds texture, grounds the image
- **Composition rules** — rule of thirds, leading lines, framing
- **Depth of field** — selective focus, bokeh, layered planes
- **Color grading** — not raw, always processed with intent
- **Narrative implication** — every image tells a story mid-scene

### Accent Colors — The Fantasy Warmth
| Name | Hex | Usage |
|------|-----|-------|
| Dragon Gold | `#FFD700` | Flame, treasure, power, dragon eyes |
| Solar Crimson | `#FF4500` | Violence, passion, danger, old magic |
| Blaze Orange | `#FF8C00` | Fire, warmth in bright scenes, energy |
| Sunlit Bronze | `#DAA520` | Artifacts, aged metal, history |

### Color Usage Rules
1. **70% Bright Tones** — Solar demands radiance as default
2. **20% Cool Accents** — Punctuate, don't saturate
3. **10% Deep Shadows** — Fantasy depth breaks the blaze
4. **Complementary tension** — Gold/cyan, orange/violet create visual friction
5. **Saturate foregrounds** — Desaturated distant haze makes elements pop

## 3. Lighting Guide

Lighting is the soul of DragonPunk Solar. Master these techniques:

### ☀️ **High-Key Solar** (Primary Technique)
Extreme contrast between blinding light and defined shadows from the sun.
```
Prompt fragment: "high-key solar lighting, single sun source, defined dramatic shadows, radiant contrast, face half-illuminated"
```
*Use for:* Character portraits, dramatic reveals, triumphant moments

### 💡 **Single Source / Hard Sunlight**
One dominant sun creates sculptural shadows with sharp edges.
```
Prompt fragment: "single sun source lighting, hard shadows, sharp shadow edges, dramatic solar lighting, overhead blaze effect"
```
*Use for:* Confrontation scenes, lone figures, power dynamics

### 🟡 **Solar Wash**
Colored light from panels and mirrors bleeds across the scene, tinting everything.
```
Prompt fragment: "solar wash lighting, gold and amber color spill, sunlit surfaces reflecting radiance, cyberpunk glow, multiple mirrored light sources"
```
*Use for:* Street scenes, urban environments, establishing shots

### 🔥 **Practical Lighting + Magic**
Light sources visible in frame (lanterns, screens, dragon flame, solar crystals).
```
Prompt fragment: "practical lighting, visible light sources, magical solar illumination, panel glow, mixed color temperature"
```
*Use for:* Interior scenes, solar forges, workshops, dragon encounters

### ✨ **Rim Lighting / Silhouette**
Back-lighting from sun creates glowing edges on figures.
```
Prompt fragment: "dramatic rim lighting, backlit silhouette against sun, glowing edges, figure against bright sky, lens flare halo"
```
*Use for:* Dramatic entrances, heroic figures, dragon reveals

### 🌤️ **Volumetric / Atmospheric**
Light catches particles in the air — dust, heat haze, pollen, steam.
```
Prompt fragment: "volumetric lighting, god rays through haze, sunbeams through dust, atmospheric shimmer, particles catching light"
```
*Use for:* Outdoor scenes, industrial areas, mystical moments

### 🌤️ **High Key Dominance**
Bright areas dominate with shadows for depth.
```
Prompt fragment: "high contrast lighting, pools of shadow in brilliance, dominant high key, selective shadows, solar contrast"
```
*Use for:* Complex scenes, establishing shots, narrative moments

### Lighting Don'ts
- ❌ Flat, even lighting (kills the solar drama)
- ❌ Dim overcast without sun play
- ❌ Multiple soft sources (loses intensity)
- ❌ Pure cold light (always tint warmly)

## 6. Model Recommendations

### 🥇 **Midjourney v6+** — Best Overall
- **Strengths:** Composition, lighting, atmosphere, style consistency
- **Best for:** All DragonPunk Solar scenes, especially establishing shots
- **Settings:** Use `--style raw` for sharper results, `--ar 16:9` or `--ar 21:9` for cinematic
- **Tips:** 
  - Use `--no [negatives]` for exclusions
  - Chain with `--sref` style references for consistency
  - `::2` weight on "dragonpunk solar" to emphasize

### 🥈 **Stable Diffusion XL + Fine-Tunes**
- **Recommended Models:**
  - **DreamShaper XL** — Excellent fantasy-realism blend
  - **Juggernaut XL** — Strong cinematic quality  
  - **RealVisXL** — Grounded realism with stylization
- **Best for:** Character portraits, detailed close-ups
- **Tips:**
  - Use LoRAs for solarpunk lighting
  - CFG 7-9 for balanced adherence
  - Add "masterpiece, best quality" to start (SDXL responds to this)

### 🥉 **DALL-E 3**
- **Strengths:** Prompt adherence, detail, accessibility
- **Limitations:** Less control over specific style, can trend saturated
- **Best for:** Quick concepts, specific scene descriptions
- **Tips:**
  - Be very explicit about brightness and solar lighting
  - Describe what you DON'T want in natural language
  - Request "cinematic film still" framing

### **Flux.1 Pro**
- **Strengths:** Excellent composition, prompt following
- **Best for:** Complex scenes, multiple elements
- **Tips:** 
  - Describe exhaustively — Flux rewards detail
  - Specify lighting explicitly

## 8. Advanced Techniques

### Style Consistency Across Series
When creating multiple images for the same project:
1. Create a "style anchor" image first
2. Reference it with style-reference features (Midjourney `--sref`, SDXL style LoRAs)
3. Maintain same lighting descriptors across prompts
4. Keep color palette explicit and consistent
5. Use same negative prompt block

### Blending Fantasy and Tech
The key to DragonPunk Solar is **integration, not juxtaposition**:
- ❌ "Dragon standing next to a solar panel"
- ✅ "Dragon's scales channeling solar energy, photovoltaic veins glowing"
- ❌ "Medieval castle with solar signs"
- ✅ "Gothic stonework evolved over centuries, original gargoyles now solar reflectors, new holographic guild banners shimmering where cloth once hung"

### Lighting for Mood
| Mood | Primary Light | Secondary Light | Shadow Density |
|------|--------------|-----------------|----------------|
| Mysterious | Single sun, off-frame | Distant mirror ambient | Defined (40-50%) |
| Dangerous | Red/amber practicals | Cyan solar rim | Medium (30-40%) |
| Optimistic | Bright ambient | Gold window glow | Light (20-30%) |
| Powerful | Dramatic uplighting | Gold accent | Defined (40-50%) |
| Chaotic | Multiple flaring sources | Fire/explosion | Irregular, shifting |

### Species Rendering Notes
- **Dragons (true form):** Emphasize scale iridescence, eye luminosity, heat effects. Scales should gleam like sunlit gems.
- **Dragons (humanoid form):** Subtle tells—unusual eye glow, scale sheen, too-perfect poise, radiant stillness.
- **Humans:** Sun-weathered, augmented, marked by intense lives. Rarely soft, often striking.
- **Other species:** Ground in solar tropes—orcs as builders, elves as engineers, etc.—then subvert expectations.
